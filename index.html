A Coach Who Has Lived It

Ashish Kulkarni is an ICF-certified Executive Coach, dedicated to helping individuals navigate transitions, sharpen decision-making, and grow into the next version of themselves. His coaching practice is grounded in deep listening, structured reflection, and a belief that every leader already holds the answers — his role is to help them find clarity and act with confidence.

He works primarily with senior professionals and emerging leaders who are facing pivotal moments in their careers: stepping into bigger roles, managing complex teams, or redefining what success means to them.

What sets his coaching apart is the lived experience behind it. Alongside his ICF credential, Ashish brings over 20+ years of senior leadership experience in global banking, having led major digital transformation and customer experience initiatives across India, Singapore, the UAE, and beyond. This gives his coaching a practical, real-world edge — he understands the pressures of high-stakes leadership because he has lived them, not just studied them. His approach blends empathetic coaching with a strategist's clarity, helping clients move from feeling stuck to feeling purposeful. Clients describe him as insightful, grounded, and genuinely invested in their growth. Ashish continues to build a space where individuals can pause, reflect, and reconnect with what matters most — to them and to the people they lead.
