Coaching That Transforms

I am an ICF Certified Coach and founder of Samaritan Coaching, partnering with leaders to unlock meaningful, lasting growth. My aim is simple: to provide coaching that inspires and guides motivated, passionate people toward their best life. Over 20+ years leading large-scale digital and AI transformations across global banking — from Standard Chartered in Singapore to Emirates NBD in Dubai — I have built a career on translating complexity into clarity, and ambition into measurable outcomes. That same discipline now shapes my coaching practice. I bring executive depth and human-centred presence to every conversation, with you firmly at the centre. Coaching, to me, is not about supplying answers; it is about creating the conditions for you to discover your own truth, sharpen your direction, and act with conviction. Whether you are navigating a leadership transition, stepping into a bigger role, or seeking deeper alignment between your values and your work, I offer a structured yet deeply personal space for reflection, insight, and intentional change. My approach blends strategic rigour with mindful inquiry — helping driven individuals move beyond goals toward genuine self-actualization, sustained well beyond our sessions together.
